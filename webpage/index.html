Overview

TigerNav is a virtual assistant for indoor navigation built on an autoregressive decision model. High-level user goals are translated into a sequence of subgoals and low-level actions, enabling purposeful movement through corridors, rooms, and multi-floor facilities while remaining simple to deploy.
